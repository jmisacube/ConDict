Luskei Numbers

Numbers in Luskei are very straightforward. Larger numbers are created by stacking smaller numbers (237 = two hundred three ten seven). There are no irregular names for numbers 11-19. Ordinal numbers, AKA "first," "fourth," etc., have a regular suffix, -na.
0 Zéro 10 Dékit 21 Dwa dékit od
1 Od 20 Dwa dékit 47 Cxit dékit sem
2 Dwa 30 Troi dékit 111 Set dékit od
3 Troi ... ... 599 Pwet set zxévit dékit zxévit
4 Cxit 100 Set 2,020 Dwa txest dwa dékit
5 Pwet 1,000 Txest 10,505 Dékit txest pwet set pwet
6 Sxes 1,000,000 Mílin
7 Sem 1,000,000,000 Bílin
8 Ósem
9 Zxévit

*N.B.: Since consonants are never (or extremely rarely) doubled in Luskei, the extra "n" here is deleted.
