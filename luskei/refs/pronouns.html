Luskei Pronouns

Pronouns in Luskei follow different declension rules from nouns. The below tables illustrate all conjugated forms of all pronouns.
Note that for 3rd person singular pronouns, only xe, zxe, txe, and at are listed; others can be invented as needed, and assuming they are similar to these three, they will conjugate similarly.
Also note that the 3rd person pronoun at is intended to be used for inanimate objects only, and is rude to use to refer to another as in English, unless requested.
